WEOS App Store — Developer Terms & Conditions
Last updated: February 2026 · Version 1.0
1.
Revenue Share. Developers receive 80% of all revenue generated by their apps. WEOS retains 20% as a platform fee. Payouts are processed monthly via Stripe Connect or UPI, minimum ₹500.
2.
App Review. All apps must pass WEOS review before publishing. WEOS reserves the right to reject or remove any app that violates community standards, contains malware, or misrepresents functionality.
3.
Data Privacy. Apps may only access member data explicitly consented to. No selling, sharing, or monetizing member data with third parties. Violation results in immediate removal and account termination.
4.
Intellectual Property. Developers retain full ownership of their app code. By publishing, you grant WEOS a non-exclusive license to display, distribute, and promote your app within the platform.
5.
Prohibited Apps. Apps that facilitate spam, gambling, adult content, pyramid schemes, financial fraud, or any illegal activity are strictly prohibited and will be permanently banned.
6.
Support SLA. Developers must respond to critical bug reports within 48 hours and provide a working support channel. Repeated failure to maintain apps results in delisting.
7.
Updates & Versioning. Apps must maintain backward compatibility. Breaking changes require a 30-day notice to affected hub owners before deployment.
8.
Termination. Either party may terminate the developer agreement with 30 days notice. WEOS may terminate immediately for serious violations. Upon termination, all apps are delisted and pending payouts are released within 60 days.
9.
Governing Law. These terms are governed by Indian law. Disputes shall be resolved through arbitration in Mumbai, Maharashtra.
10.
Changes to Terms. WEOS may update these terms with 14 days notice via email. Continued publishing after notice constitutes acceptance.
